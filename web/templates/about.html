About Bookmark App

Our Mission

Bookmark App is designed to help you organize and manage your web resources efficiently. We believe in creating a simple yet powerful tool that enhances your browsing experience and helps you keep track of important websites.

Our goal is to provide a clean, intuitive interface that makes bookmarking effortless and accessible from anywhere.

Key Features

  • Simple and intuitive bookmark management
  • Fast access to your favorite websites
  • Responsive design for all devices
  • Clean and modern user interface
  • Secure storage of your bookmarks

Technology Stack

  • Built with Rust for performance and reliability
  • Styled with Tailwind CSS for modern design
  • Enhanced with HTMX for dynamic interactions
  • Responsive layout for desktop and mobile
  • Optimized for speed and efficiency
